The Work

Therapeutic, dramatic performance art — a lyrical journey or monologue & melody: looping piano and song.

This work is personal and cathartic — but in delivery becomes universal. The perspective broadens. I find myself more careful for the audience's sake than I ever am for my own. I still want to be honest, and inspire — but because of the privilege of doing so this way, getting to explore ideas in a way that makes a connection, you come to know and inspire yourself better, and more gently. It is an alchemy that feels like it comes from above.

For Whom & Where

The Right Room Matters

This is not background music. It is not a dinner show. It is a performance that asks something of its audience — and gives a great deal more in return.

About the Performer

Paul Tosh

P

Born in Scotland. Formed in Europe. Seasoned across a decade of performances for senior communities on the American East Coast. Paul Tosh is a therapeutic musical performer whose work refuses the easy distinction between entertainment and art.

His signature approach — Song Is The Play — treats every performance as a piece of theatre: looping piano, dramatic song, and the kind of presence that makes a familiar melody feel heard for the first time. Paper Gun is his most personal and fully realised work to date: a show about damage, clarity, and the quiet violence of the truth.
